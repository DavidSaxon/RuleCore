You are the blue team, destroy the red teams artillery units using your own.

Right click on a blue unit to change it's attack rule:

    - a white circle/ring above the unit means it will not attack

    - a purple/pink downwards arrow above the unit means it's attacks will be
        destroying terrain in the world

    - a red/orange upwards arrow above the unit means it's attacks will be
        adding terrain to the world

Left click and drag on your units to rotate them change their angle of fire



- Hold middle click and drag to rotate the camera

- Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out

- Hold shift and middle click to translate the camera ( buggy )

- Hold shift and scroll to change the elevation of the camera
